Our Services

We offer a variety of services to meet the needs of farmers, businesses, and consumers. Below are some of our key offerings:

Entrepreneur Development Program

Empowering rural youth and women by establishing soil testing labs and agricultural product outlets, boosting productivity and entrepreneurship.

Urban Gardening Solutions

Bagwani World offers customized solutions for kitchen, terrace, and home gardens, with specialized products for healthier plants.

Comprehensive Agricultural Solutions

Providing high-quality, research-backed agricultural products to enhance yield, soil health, and sustainability through a strong distributor and dealers network.

Agricultural, Environmental, Livelihood, Consultancy

Expert consultancy in sustainable farming, soil health, livelihood promotion, FPO/SHG strengthening, waste management, NABARD, NGOs, and CSR-driven initiatives.

Carbon Footprint Reduction & Renewable Energy

Promoting biogas from cow dung, organic composting, afforestation, and farmer training to support carbon credit benefits and sustainability.
